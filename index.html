I'm a Ph.D. student in the Ecology, Evolution, and Marine Biology Department at UC Santa Barbara. My research interests include using Nanopore sequencing in order to gain new insights into the ecology and evolution of complex biological samples from marine environments. I'm passionate about all things -omics, and my favorite programming language is Python. I love to do and talk science with anyone who would humor me.
